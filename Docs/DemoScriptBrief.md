# Demo Video Script: Blazor Book Library (Brief Version) 📚⚡️

**Tone**: Fast-paced, informal, direct.

---

## 1. Intro
"Hey! Welcome to the **Blazor Book Library**. I'm showing you how we've combined professional archiving with AI power."

## 2. AI Semantic Search
"As a user, I’m not stuck with keywords anymore. I can search for books by *meaning*. I’ll type: *'a story about human resilience during war'*. The AI understands the context and finds the most relevant matches in our archive instantly."

## 3. Automated Curation
"Switching to manager mode. Adding a book is a breeze. I just enter the ISBN, hit 'Search API', and the system pulls all metadata and covers from Google Books. No more manual entry errors."

## 4. AI Embeddings
"To power that semantic search, I can generate AI embeddings for any book description with one click. I can even perform a 'Sanity Check' to verify the AI's understanding of the content."

## 5. Wrap Up
"Clean, fast, and intelligent. That’s the Modern Archivist. Thanks for watching!"
